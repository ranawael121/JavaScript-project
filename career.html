OPERATIONS & SUPPLIES TEAM LEADER

Job Summary:

A talented and dynamic person, with a passion to innovate and contribute in a fast-paced growing environment. Operations & Supplies Team Leader acts as bridge between business and operations teams, also is responsible for maximizing the process efficiency and productivity, through all phases of production scheduling, inventory control, purchasing of raw materials.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and lead proper execution of Operations planning strategies to reach optimal operational efficiencies.
  • Oversees material capacity, reviews material usage, and supplier management.
  • Driving efforts to significantly improve physical inventory management practices and accuracy.
  • Oversee all purchase orders, adjust purchase patterns, and monitor shipment and production schedules to meet sales requirements.
  • Evaluate vendor performance to ensure the lowest cost, highest quality, and timely deliveries.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in any related field.
  • Enthusiastic leader able to drive results and build a strong team.
  • Team player who is proactive and has a passion to be on this field.
  • Has good problem solving and analytical skills.
  • Flexible and adapts easily with rapidly changing working schedules.
  • Organized and detail oriented.
